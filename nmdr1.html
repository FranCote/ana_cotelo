NMDR1

WORK ABOUT PRINTS

CONVERSATIONAL FURNITURE


NMDR1 is inspired by the ‘Tête à Tête’ seat, which was created in the 19th century, with the intention of connecting its users.

It is a place to sit and talk, eye-to-eye, face- to-face. The distance between the seats is reduced to generate more privacy and a comfortable space to talk.


The possibility of having two different seats is realized in this concept.The idea is therefore more versatile and adaptable to different spaces than a conventional chair.
