This is the result of my spare time and the best way I find to express myself. These are digital illustrations made from my original artwork. The original pointillism illustrations were made in ink and pen.


The hand draw illustrations were inspired on the classic sculpture “Le Baiser” by Auguste Rodin.


The posters are printed on high-quality paper (Chunky 200-250 gsm) and are available in A5 and A4 format.
