ESSENTIAL FURNITURE


The chair is inspired by Jean Prouve’s industrial design concepts. Prouve´s projects priorities functionality and usability, taking advantage of the technologies and materials available at the time.


The idea is to make the structure an important visual component that is not hidden, but part of the whole piece’s aesthetic.


This is complemented by the simplicity of the wood, intentionally highlighting each material’s beauty.
